About Us

Welcome to Miyagi's Cat Café 🐾 At Miyagi Cat Café, everything starts with a simple love for cats. Here, you can visit the café, enjoy a fika, and spend time meeting our lovely cats. We believe every cat deserves a safe home, endless love, and a place where they truly belong. That’s why we focus on helping them find caring families who will welcome them with warmth and kindness. From shy little souls to playful adventurers, every cat deserves to feel loved, protected, and at home. Miyagi Cat Café isn’t just a café for a fika — it’s a place built on passion, personality, and a soft spot for whiskers, paws, and sleepy little purrs. It’s also a space where you can adopt and give these cats a new, loving home. Thank you for being part of our journey 💛
